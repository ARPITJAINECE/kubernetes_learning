1. What Is a Persistent Volume (PV)?
A Persistent Volume (PV) is a cluster-level storage resource that exists independently of Pods.

Think of it as:
“A hard disk plugged into the cluster.”






2. What Is a Persistent Volume Claim (PVC)?
A Persistent Volume Claim (PVC) is a request for storage made by a Pod.

Think of it as:
“I want 10Gi storage with ReadWriteOnce access.”
