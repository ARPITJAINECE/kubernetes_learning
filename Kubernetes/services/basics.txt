A Service in Kubernetes is a stable networking abstraction that exposes a group of Pods under a single, consistent IP address and DNS name, even if Pods change over time.


‚öôÔ∏è Key Points (Crisp Basics)
Pods are ephemeral ‚Äî they get recreated, changing IPs.
A Service provides a permanent endpoint to access those Pods.
It routes traffic to Pods using label selectors.
Ensures load balancing across healthy Pods.
Can expose Pods internally or externally based on type.
Decouples applications from dynamic Pod IPs.
Supports discovery, scaling, and stable connectivity within the cluster.
