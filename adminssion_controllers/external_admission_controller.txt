2. How External Admission Controllers work (one-line)
Kubernetes sends the resource request to an external HTTP(S) service, waits for its response, and then either accepts, rejects, or mutates the request.



3. Why External Admission Controllers exist

Built-in admission controllers:
    Are generic
    Are limited
    Cannot handle custom business rules

External admission controllers solve:
    Custom security rules
    Organization-specific policies
    Compliance requirements
